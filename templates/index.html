Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Memoir

What is Memoir? +

Memoir is a voice-powered AI tool that turns your spoken memories into a written life story. You speak your memories into the mic — Memoir transcribes your words and AI weaves them into chapters of your life, written in your own voice.

How do I write my life story? +

You don't have to write anything. Just speak. Open the Voice Studio, tap the mic, and share a memory — any memory. Memoir transcribes it and AI organizes your stories into memoir chapters automatically. No writing experience needed.

Can AI write a memoir for me? +

Yes. You provide the raw material — your voice, your stories, your truth. Memoir's AI shapes it into prose that sounds like you, not a robot. Think of it as a co-author that never forgets anything you say.

How do I preserve family memories? +

Memoir is built for exactly this. Speak stories about your childhood, your parents, your family moments. Each session is saved and woven into a permanent record you can share with future generations — something that outlasts any photo album.

Is Memoir free? +

Yes, free to use. No account required to get started. Built by Creative Konsoles.
